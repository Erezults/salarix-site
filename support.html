Frequently asked questions

How do I add a payslip?

Tap the “+ Add payslip” button on the home screen. You can type the details manually, or import a payslip image/PDF and let AI fill the fields for you.

How does AI payslip import work?

Take a photo, scan, or import a PDF of your payslip. Salarix uses Google Gemini to extract the amounts and dates, then pre-fills the form so you can review and edit before saving.

What are “files”?

Files let you track multiple people or jobs separately (e.g. you and a spouse). Each file has its own payslips, managers, and charts. You can rename any file in the Files screen.

What is raise attribution?

When Salarix detects a salary increase, you can attribute it to a source — manager, union, index-linking, or role change — to understand what's driving your pay over time. Open Analytics and tap a detected raise.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes. Salarix is offline-first — add and edit payslips without a connection, and everything syncs automatically when you reconnect.

How do I export my data?

Go to Settings → Data → Export and choose JSON, PDF, or a password-encrypted file.

Is my salary data private?

Yes. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and protected with Row-Level Security so only you can access it. No ads, no trackers. See our Privacy Policy.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are billed through Apple. Manage or cancel anytime in the App Store: Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data and cannot be undone. You can also request deletion by emailing support@erezults.com.

I forgot my password

On the sign-in screen, tap “Forgot password?” and enter your email. You'll receive a reset link within a few minutes — check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive.

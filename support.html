Frequently asked questions

Is Salarix free?

Yes — Salarix is free to use, always, within the Free plan limits (one file, including AI payslip import). Pro and Business unlock analytics, export and more files. Prices are shown in the App Store, where purchases are made securely through Apple.

How do I add a payslip?

Tap “+ Add payslip” on the home screen. Type the details manually, or import a payslip image/PDF and let AI fill them for you.

How does AI import work?

Take a photo, scan, or import a PDF. Salarix uses Google Gemini to extract the amounts and dates and pre-fills the form so you can review and edit before saving.

Can I import several payslips at once?

Yes. Batch import lets you select multiple payslip images or PDFs — AI processes them all and you review each one before saving.

What are “files”?

Files let you track multiple people or jobs separately, each with its own payslips, managers, and charts. You can rename any file in the Files screen.

What is raise attribution?

When Salarix detects a salary increase, you can attribute it to a source — manager, union, index-linking, or role change — to understand what's driving your pay. Open Analytics and tap a detected raise.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes. Salarix is offline-first — add and edit payslips without a connection, and everything syncs automatically when you reconnect.

How do I export my data?

Go to Settings → Data → Export and choose JSON, PDF, or a password-encrypted file.

Is my salary data private?

Yes. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and protected with Row-Level Security so only you can access it. No ads, no trackers. See our Privacy Policy.

I deleted something by mistake — can I get it back?

Yes. Deleted payslips and files go to the built-in trash, where you can restore them. Open Settings → Trash to recover deleted data.

Does Salarix work on Mac?

Yes. Salarix is available for both iPhone and Mac, with the same account and automatic sync between your devices.

Can I lock the app with Face ID?

Yes. Turn on “Biometric lock (Face ID)” in Settings, and Salarix will require Face ID (or your device passcode) every time it opens.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are billed through Apple. Manage or cancel anytime in the App Store: Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data and cannot be undone. You can also request deletion by emailing salarix.support@erezults.com.

I forgot my password

On the sign-in screen, tap “Forgot password?” and enter your email. You'll get a reset link within minutes — check spam if it doesn't arrive.

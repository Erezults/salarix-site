Accessibility Statement

Last updated: 14 July 2026

Salarix (operated by Erez Cohen / Erezults) is committed to making this website accessible to people with disabilities. This website was built and reviewed to conform to Israeli Standard 5568 (based on WCAG 2.1), Level AA, in accordance with the Equal Rights for Persons with Disabilities Law, 5758-1998.

Accessibility measures on this site

  • Descriptive alternative text (alt) on all informative images.
  • Text and background colors meet a minimum contrast ratio of 4.5:1.
  • Full keyboard navigation: every link, button and field can be reached and operated with Tab alone, with a visible focus indicator and a “Skip to content” link.
  • An accessibility toolbar (bottom corner, ♿ icon) to increase text size, switch to a high-contrast theme, and stop animations.
  • Animations respect the operating system's “reduce motion” setting, and pause automatically in that mode.
  • Content is fully available in Hebrew and English, with correct RTL/LTR text direction for each language.

Known limitations

We are not aware of any accessibility barriers on this site at this time. If you encounter a difficulty, please let us know using the contact details below and we will address it.

Reporting an accessibility issue

If any part of this site is not accessible to you, please email the accessibility coordinator above with a description of the issue, the page URL, and the device/browser you used. We take accessibility feedback seriously and will work to fix confirmed issues.

What it does

Understand your salary, over time

Everything you need to see where your pay has been — and where it's going.

🤖

AI payslip import

Snap or import a payslip (image or PDF) and AI fills in the fields. Review and edit before saving.

📈

Charts & insights

Net/gross trends, bonus %, overtime, and year-over-year change — in clean, readable charts.

📊

Raise attribution

Detect salary increases and attribute them to a source — manager, union, index, or role.

🗂️

Multiple files

Track several people or jobs separately, each with its own managers and colored charts.

🔒

Private by design

Your data is encrypted and accessible only to you (Row-Level Security). No ads, no trackers.

📤

Own your data

Export everything as JSON, PDF, or a password-encrypted file — anytime, no lock-in.

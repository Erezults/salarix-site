What it does

Understand your salary, over time

Everything you need to see where your pay has been — and where it's going.

🤖

AI payslip import

Snap or import a payslip (image or PDF) — or a whole batch at once — and AI fills in the fields. Review and edit before saving. Included free.

📈

Charts & insights

Net/gross trends, bonus %, overtime, and year-over-year comparison — in clean, readable charts with smart insights.

📊

Raise attribution

Detect salary increases and attribute them to a source — manager, union, index, or role — to see what really drives your pay.

🗂️

Multiple files

Track several people or jobs separately, each with its own payslips, managers and colored charts.

🔒

Private & secure

Face ID app lock, encryption in transit and at rest, and Row-Level Security so only you can access your data. No ads, no trackers.

📤

Own your data

Export everything as JSON, a designed PDF report, or a password-encrypted file — anytime, no lock-in. Deleted something? Restore it from the built-in trash.

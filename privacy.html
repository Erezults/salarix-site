Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 June 2026

Salarix (“the app”, “we”) is a personal salary-tracking app for iPhone, operated by Erez Cohen / Erezults. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your rights. We do not sell your data, show ads, or use third-party advertising trackers.

Information we collect

  • Account. When you sign up with email or Sign in with Apple, we store your email address and display name to create and secure your account.
  • Salary data you enter. Payslip details you add — gross/net pay, bonuses, overtime, manager, month, notes — and the files you group them into.
  • Payslip media you import. If you import a payslip image or PDF, the file is stored privately and linked to that record so you can view it later.
  • Purchases. If you subscribe, Apple processes the payment. We never see your card details — only your subscription tier and Apple’s transaction identifier.

AI payslip parsing

When you choose to import a payslip, the image or PDF is sent over an encrypted connection to Google’s Gemini API solely to extract the text fields that pre-fill the form. The file is processed transiently and is not used to train AI models. You always review and can edit the values before saving — or enter everything manually and never use AI.

How your data is stored & protected

  • Data is stored on Supabase (PostgreSQL), encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest.
  • Row-Level Security ensures each record is accessible only to the account that owns it. Payslip files live in a private bucket restricted to the owner.
  • The app is offline-first: your data is cached on your device and syncs securely when you reconnect.

Third-party services

  • Supabase — authentication, database and file storage.
  • Google Gemini — on-demand text extraction from payslips you import.
  • Apple — Sign in with Apple and In-App Purchases.

Your rights & choices

  • Export. Export all your data anytime from Settings → Data (JSON, PDF, or a password-encrypted file).
  • Deletion. Delete your account and all data from Settings → Account → Delete Account. This is permanent. You may also request deletion by email.
  • Access & correction. All your data is visible and editable inside the app.

Retention & children

We keep your data while your account is active; deleting your account removes your records and payslip files. Salarix is not directed at children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Contact

Questions or requests? Email salarix.support@erezults.com.

Privacy Policy

Your salary data stays yours

Salarix (“the app”, “we”) is a personal salary-tracking app for iPhone, operated by Erez Cohen / Erezults. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your rights. We do not sell your data, show ads, or use third-party advertising trackers.

Last updated: 27 June 2026

Information we collect

  • Account. When you sign up with email or Sign in with Apple, we store your email address and display name to create and secure your account.
  • Salary data you enter. Payslip details you add — gross/net pay, bonuses, overtime hours, manager, month, and notes — and the files you group them into.
  • Payslip media you import. If you import a payslip image or PDF, the file is stored privately and linked to that record so you can view it later.
  • Purchases. If you subscribe, Apple processes the payment. We never see your card details — we only store your subscription tier and Apple’s transaction identifier to unlock features.

AI payslip parsing

When you choose to import a payslip, the image or PDF is sent over an encrypted connection to Google’s Gemini API solely to extract the text fields (amounts, dates, etc.) that pre-fill the form. The file is processed transiently for that request and is not used to train AI models. You always review and can edit the extracted values before saving. If you prefer, you can enter all data manually and never use AI parsing.

How your data is stored & protected

  • Data is stored on Supabase (PostgreSQL) and encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest.
  • Row-Level Security ensures each record is accessible only to the account that owns it. Payslip files live in a private storage bucket restricted to the owner.
  • The app is offline-first: your data is cached on your device and syncs securely when you reconnect.

How we use your data

Only to provide the app: to store and display your salary records, generate your charts and insights, parse payslips you import, sync across your devices, and manage your subscription entitlement. We do not use your salary data for advertising or share it with third parties for marketing.

Third-party services

  • Supabase — authentication, database and file storage.
  • Google Gemini — on-demand text extraction from payslips you choose to import.
  • Apple — Sign in with Apple and In-App Purchases.

Your rights & choices

  • Export. Export all your data at any time from Settings → Data (JSON, PDF, or a password-encrypted file).
  • Deletion. Delete your account and all associated data from Settings → Account → Delete Account. This is permanent and cannot be undone. You may also request deletion by emailing us.
  • Access/correction. All your data is visible and editable inside the app.

Data retention

We keep your data while your account is active. When you delete your account, your records and payslip files are removed.

Children

Salarix is not directed at children under 16 and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with a new “Last updated” date.

Contact

Questions or requests? Email support@erezults.com.
